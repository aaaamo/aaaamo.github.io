Zenerate App

Date 2021 - 2024
Type Professional Work
Role Lead Computational Designer
Office Zenerate
Tech Stack python, Node.js, mySQL, QGIS, git

The Zenerate App is a cloud-based, automated feasibility study tool for multifamily modular housing development. To explore the potential of a site, the app generates multiple solutions to maximize constraints, including floor area ratio, density, and height regulations, while adhering to building codes and design preferences.
