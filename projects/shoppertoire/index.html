Shoppertoire

Date Spring 2020
Type Undergraduate Thesis Project
Tutor John Hong

Shoppertoire envisions a future of self-governing architecture in which infrastructure serves as a platform for adaptable typologies, particularly suited to commercial programs. Located in the traditional marketplace of Namdaemun in central Seoul, this proposal introduces a flexible system of commerce, distribution, and storage that can rapidly expand, shrink, or self-correct to meet evolving demands. A network of conveyor belts, modular programs, and transparent architectural elements forms the core of the project, reflecting changing retail practices and embracing user agency in shaping the environment.
