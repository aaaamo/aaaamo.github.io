Interactive Reaction-Diffusion

Date Fall 2025
Type Group Project
Collaborators Mary Zhang, Chloe Xu

The Reaction-Diffusion System is an interactive visualization that implements the Gray-Scott model, a mathematical framework that simulates the emergent patterns formed by chemical reactions in nature. By manipulating feed and kill rates alongside diffusion coefficients, the system generates diverse organic patterns.
The project explores human-system interaction through two epochs: one responding to urban fabric imagery from cities like Pittsburgh and Barcelona, and another driven by real-time audio input that maps pitch and amplitude to spatial coordinates.
Feed-Kill ratio presets were taken from https://github.com/colejd/Reaction-Diffusion-ThreeJS
